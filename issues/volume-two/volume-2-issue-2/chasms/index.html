--- title: Chasms author: Taylor Gray Moore previous: /issues/volume-two/volume-2-issue-2/currents-prose-poem/ next: /issues/volume-two/volume-2-issue-2/to-court-unreality/ --- {% include sqs-poem-begin.html %} Last thing we learned, the street had been torn up. So we had to walk on wooden planks, over chasms, whenever we went out. But now we can see: the planks have gone, so we must walk on air to get our groceries, see our friends, or even mail our letters. {% include sqs-poem-end.html %}
